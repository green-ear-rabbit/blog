In the afternoon, the patients in the inpatient department are quiet and fall sleep. I usually go to the outpatient department, where I can talk and ask questions to the doctors when they are free.

A doctor said that:

“Many people got syphilis and are not be aware of that, but it’s not a problem. Because nowadays, people take antibiotics often, you know, even for a cold. Treponema pallidum is so easy to be killed. Many patients found they were infected until they go to blood donation.”

It’s really surprised me.

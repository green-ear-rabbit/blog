An human experiment: How does pescetarian diet affect health

2020-11-27

Introduction

To evaluate the effect the health effect of pescetarian diet, an two-month’s intervention study was conducted on a 19-year-old female, from April, 6th to July 12th, in 2019. Food frequency questionnaire were answered by the participant at baseline. Blood glucose, hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c), blood urea nitrogen (BUN), high-density lipoprotein (HDL), low-density lipoprotein (LDL), height and body weight were measured at both baseline and follow-up.

Limitations

  • The sample size is only one, because the author fail to find more volunteer to participate in this study.

  • It is possible that certain genetic predisposition influences the appetite of fresh, and also influence the health outcomes.
